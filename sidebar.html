Note: See 'data' sheet for example.
  1. Have a sheet with only two columns.
  2. Mark categories with colored cells, if applicable.
  3. List items and pricing underneath the categories. Items in the first column, pricing in the second column.
    • Pricing should be formatted in currency, ie. $XXX.XX. Use the format tool.
  4. Scripts use your active sheet. Go to the sheet with pricing data before trying to run scripts.
  5. Click on the shortcut buttons to add or remove Pikachu shortcuts from the sheet.
  6. Click on the surprised Pikachu images to automatically generate a new sheet with either expanding rows or columns.
    • The new sheet will be automatically named to the current day and time (in PST) in the format of yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss. Feel free to rename.
    • On the newly created sheets, the items are grouped under categories. You can expand the corresponding row or column to see the items.
    • For each item, there is a checkbox. Select the desired checkbox(es). In the 'Total' row or column, the sum of the items should appear.
